bakery · in-residence programs

residences for young global entrepreneurs building global brands.

bakery is softerbread's residence layer for young builders across africa, latin america, the caribbean, and the diaspora — we call them bakers in residence. we support bakers turning ideas, skills, culture, and community insight into brands, tools, services, and ventures with global potential.

bakery also supports builders connected to other birthright ventures, giving each baker access to the same practical infrastructure: room + board where available, resources, technology, access, and structure.

what bakery provides
01 · room + board

a place to focus

safe, stable living support where available — especially for builders who need room to work without carrying everything else.

02 · resources

small grants + materials

operating support, starter budgets, and practical materials to get the work made where available.

03 · technology

tools + digital infrastructure

access to software, ai systems, templates, hosting, and hands-on build support.

04 · access

people + markets

mentors, collaborators, operators, buyers, partners, funders, and distribution pathways.

05 · structure

plans + reviews

clear build plans, weekly and monthly milestones, venture reviews, and documentation.

06 · opportunity-cost support

stipends where possible

for young builders who would otherwise need to pause the work to support family or cover basic obligations.
